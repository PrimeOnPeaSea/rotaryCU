Who are we

Rotaract clubs bring together people ages 18 and above to exchange ideas with leaders in the community, develop leadership and professional skills, and have fellowship through service.

In communities worldwide, Rotary and Rotaract members work side by side to take action through service. From big cities to rural villages, Rotaract is changing communities like yours.

Rotaract Fellowships

Rotaract Fellowships are groups that share a common passion. Being part of a fellowship is a fun way to make friends beyond your club, explore a hobby or profession, and enhance your Rotaract experience.
